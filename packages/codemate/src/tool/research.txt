Create a structured research workspace by writing `outline.yaml` and `fields.yaml` into a topic-specific folder.

Use this after you have already decided the research items and field categories to save. It stores the outline, execution settings, and field definitions so later research tools can continue from files instead of free-form prompts.

Use this tool when:
- The task requires multi-step investigation across several unknowns
- You need to track findings across retries instead of redoing ad-hoc searches
- Websearch alone is insufficient and you need durable, structured research outputs
- selfcheck failed and you need to re-plan with explicit findings before the next major fix

When repeated troubleshooting fails:
- After two failed attempts on one subproblem, initialize structured research before attempting a third major change.
